THE PLAN

The results are in! - Sounds like ya'll losers wanna read.

Based on the survey I've decided on a few things!...

Initially, I thought we would finish an entire book between meetings, but some people mentioned meeting more regularly. I thought that could be fun to discuss books while we progress, so I liked this idea a lot. I want this to be super casual, so there's never pressure to attend all the meetings.

My current idea/plan is to read a book a month and meet every other week for a mid-book update! Finales will be hosted at someones house/apartment, and mid-book meetups will be out on the town. We can discuss more what we want this to look like at our first meeting. I've added a survey to gather times that people are available and figured we could meetup to pick our first book and iron out the details!
